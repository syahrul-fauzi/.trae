- Generate momentum and commitment for successful Phase 2 execution

- Build team cohesion and collaborative working relationships
- Clarify decision-making processes and escalation procedures
- Confirm resource availability and commitment levels
- Establish baseline expectations for quality and performance

## 8. Risk Management & Mitigation

### 8.1 Identified Risks (Pre-Kickoff)
| Risk Category | Specific Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation Strategy |
|---------------|---------------|-------------|--------|-------------------|
| **Technical** | Integration complexity | Medium | High | Early prototyping, expert consultation |
| **Resource** | Key personnel availability | Low | High | Backup resource identification |
| **Timeline** | Third-party dependencies | Medium | Medium | Buffer time, alternative solutions |
| **Quality** | Performance requirements | Low | Medium | Early performance testing |

### 8.2 Risk Monitoring & Review
- **Weekly**: Risk register review in status meetings
- **Bi-weekly**: Detailed risk assessment with mitigation owners
- **Monthly**: Risk trend analysis and new risk identification
- **As-needed**: Immediate escalation for high-impact risks

## 10. Contingency Planning

### 10.1 Virtual Meeting Backup
- **Primary**: Hybrid meeting with in-person and virtual components
- **Backup**: Fully virtual meeting with enhanced interaction tools
- **Emergency**: Asynchronous communication with recorded presentations

### 10.2 Attendance Issues
- **Low attendance**: Reschedule within 48 hours
- **Key stakeholder absence**: Individual follow-up sessions
- **Technical difficulties**: Phone conference backup

### 10.3 Content Delivery Issues
- **Presentation failure**: Printed materials and verbal delivery
- **Technical content gaps**: Follow-up technical sessions scheduled
- **Time overruns**: Additional focused meetings scheduled
